# Tips

## Floating Window

- The list **supports scrolling** — **right-click** anywhere on the floating window to open the context menu
- The context menu lets you quickly pick which window to control, and works with most modern games

## Recording

- When recording above 4K (excluding 4K), you need to select the **H.265** encoder

## Hotkeys

- All hotkeys for the floating window, recording, and screenshots are customizable in Settings, and **mouse side buttons** are supported
